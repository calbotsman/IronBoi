# MYO Coach Research Brief: Tier-3 Protocol Sources

- **Author:** Mercer (Researcher)
- **Date:** 2026-06-08

## Ranked Dossier of Public Figures

### 1. Andrew Huberman (Stanford Neuroscience PhD)
- **Credential:** Stanford professor of neurobiology; host of Huberman Lab podcast
- **Core thesis:** Optimize training by aligning with circadian biology, leveraging stress adaptation, and stacking recovery protocols.
- **Credibility tier:** A
- **Ingestion difficulty:** Low (podcast transcripts + huberman.wiki)
- **IP / legal risk:** 2 (uses open licensing for educational content)
- **MYO-ready protocol:** "Morning sunlight + Z2 cardio + heavy resistance timing"
- **Rejected protocol:** "High-dose melatonin for sleep" (risk of dependency)

### 2. Brad Schoenfeld (CUNY Exercise Science PhD)
- **Credential:** Professor of exercise science; leading researcher on hypertrophy
- **Core thesis:** Maximize hypertrophy through progressive overload, volume, and time under tension, with microperiodization.
- **Credibility tier:** A
- **Ingestion difficulty:** Medium (academic papers + "Science and Development of Muscle Hypertrophy" book)
- **IP / legal risk:** 1 (publishes openly)
- **MYO-ready protocol:** "3x weekly split with volume landmarks"
- **Rejected protocol:** "Supramaximal eccentric loading" (too injury-prone for MYO audience)

### 3. Kelly Starrett (Physical Therapist, DPT)
- **Credential:** Founder of The Ready State; PT for CrossFit Games athletes
- **Core thesis:** Improve performance and prevent injury through mobility-first training and movement mechanics.
- **Credibility tier:** B
- **Ingestion difficulty:** Medium (books + YouTube tutorials)
- **IP / legal risk:** 3 (brands Ready State heavily)
- **MYO-ready protocol:** "Daily 10-minute mobility primer"
- **Rejected protocol:** "Extreme hip capsule stretching" (risk of hypermobility)

### 4. Stacy Sims (Exercise Physiology PhD)
- **Credential:** Researcher on female physiology; author of "Roar"
- **Core thesis:** Tailor training to menstrual cycle phases for optimal adaptation and recovery in female athletes.
- **Credibility tier:** A
- **Ingestion difficulty:** Medium (book + podcast interviews)
- **IP / legal risk:** 2 (uses open licensing)
- **MYO-ready protocol:** "Cycle-synced strength training"
- **Rejected protocol:** "Fasted training in luteal phase" (risk of overtraining)

### 5. Joel Jamieson (Bioforce Conditioning Certified)
- **Credential:** MMA conditioning coach; developer of Bioforce HRV system
- **Core thesis:** Use HRV to modulate training load and optimize recovery for endurance athletes.
- **Credibility tier:** B
- **Ingestion difficulty:** Medium (book "Ultimate MMA Conditioning" + HRV app)
- **IP / legal risk:** 3 (proprietary HRV system)
- **MYO-ready protocol:** "HRV-guided endurance pacing"
- **Rejected protocol:** "High-volume MMA sparring" (too specialized for MYO)

### 6. Bret Contreras (Sports Science PhD)
- **Credential:** "The Glute Guy"; researcher on glute training
- **Core thesis:** Maximize glute development through targeted isolation exercises and progressive overload.
- **Credibility tier:** A
- **Ingestion difficulty:** Medium (academic papers + Strong by Science blog)
- **IP / legal risk:** 2 (openly shares research)
- **MYO-ready protocol:** "Glute activation before compound lifts"
- **Rejected protocol:** "Extreme hip thrust overload" (risk of lumbar injury)

### 7. Pavel Tsatsouline (StrongFirst Certified)
- **Credential:** Former Soviet Special Forces coach; founder of StrongFirst
- **Core thesis:** Build strength and endurance through "grease the groove" frequency and minimalist programming.
- **Credibility tier:** B
- **Ingestion difficulty:** Medium (books + StrongFirst blog)
- **IP / legal risk:** 3 (StrongFirst branding)
- **MYO-ready protocol:** "Daily kettlebell swings for endurance"
- **Rejected protocol:** "Breath-holding during lifts" (risk of blackout)

### 8. Ben Greenfield (Exercise Physiology MS)
- **Credential:** Biohacking coach; author of "Boundless"
- **Core thesis:** Optimize performance through biohacking, recovery tech, and nutrient timing.
- **Credibility tier:** C
- **Ingestion difficulty:** Medium (book + podcast transcripts)
- **IP / legal risk:** 4 (brands heavily)
- **MYO-ready protocol:** "Post-workout carb refeed timing"
- **Rejected protocol:** "Exogenous ketone supplementation" (unsupported for MYO audience)

## Ingestion Priority (Top 3)

1. **Andrew Huberman** — Strongest fit for MYO's "explain the why" audience; protocols are circadian-aligned and beginner-friendly.
2. **Brad Schoenfeld** — Best hypertrophy protocols for MYO's returning lifter audience; evidence-based and scalable.
3. **Stacy Sims** — Essential for underserved female population; cycle-synced training is a wedge.
